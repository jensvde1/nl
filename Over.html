About

In 2013, Jens VDE obtained the diploma print media at Viso-Mariakerke. After that he studied a specialization year and obtained the diploma as a graphic designer, screen printer and print and signer. After years of graphic arts, he decided to deepen in art more specifically drawing.

He is currently studying at Sint-lucas visual arts Gent. A third bachelor's degree program drawing and printmaking. Places and spaces that triggers and fascinates him bring, he brings in picture. Travelling and locations that happen by coincidence are important for his work. His drawings range from truthful and abstract images. He mainly signs in charcoal in combination with other materials. Layering, Contrast, Shadow, Light and Dark are priority in his work.
 
In 2016 - 2017 he went deeper into digital art, new media and sculpture. Mistakes in an image, glitches or bugs, he considers to be aesthetic, a malfunction in an image. He does not only go digital in this way, but also in 3D. In these images the balance between figurative and abstraction is very clear. On the one hand, an image provides for interpretation by abstraction, and on the other hand, there is a hold on to reality of referring to a place or happening. The interplay between figurative and abstraction often gives an distorted feeling and shows a world, thought or feeling that he has in mind.
